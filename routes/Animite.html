Animite

Animite is an Anilist client for Android. It helps users track their anime & manga lists.

Stack

The app tries to make use of the latest Android libraries and APIs:

  1. Architecture: MVVM
  2. Networking:
    • Apollo Kotlin
    • Kotlin Coroutines
    • COIL
  3. UI/UX:
    • Made purely with Jetpack Compose
    • Assets with Figma
